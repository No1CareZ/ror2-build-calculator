CALCULATOR GUIDELINE

Tips which will let you understand how to use calculator:

ITEM SELECTION

Select your items by typing in amount of them. Be aware 0 is a minum 256 is a maximum just like in game!

LOAD AND SAVE OPTION

Use "Copy to clipboard" button to save your loadout to clipboard. Be aware calculator only saves your level and amount of items, it dosent save difficulty!

Use "Load loadout" button with text-input tab to load your previously saved loadout to clipboard

STARTING CALUCLATION PROCESS

Use "Calculate" button to see success-rate of your loadout
